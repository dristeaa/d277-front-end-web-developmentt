Virginia Beach, Virginia

Virginia Beach is a popular oceanfront city with a military facility presence. Virginia Beach is considered to be one of the best places to start and raise families. It boasts a population greater than any other cities in the Virginia state. The food scene is also filled with diverse choices of different cultures and styles of food. Virginia Beach was established in 1906 (“About Virginia Beach," n.d.).

Virginia Beach, Virginia, has a population of 459,470 people as of the 2020 U.S Census ("Explore census data," n.d.).

The region that Virginia Beach is located in is the Coastal Plan/Tidewater region. region. Virginia Beach is urban and suburban (“About Virginia Beach,” n.d.).

Virginia Beach's average household salary is $83,245 yearly, compared to Virginia's average salary of $85,873 ("Explore census data," n.d.).
