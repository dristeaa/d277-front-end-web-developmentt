Norfolk, Virginia

Norfolk is both a seaport and a vibrant city. There are a number of festivals, attractions, restaurants, and other fun activities throughout Norfolk. Norfolk has deep roots in history with being a city for military transport. The city of Norfolk was established in the year 1682, and eventually incorporated as a city by the year 1845. The city is right next to the Atlantic Ocean, also bordering the nearby Virginia Beach city.
