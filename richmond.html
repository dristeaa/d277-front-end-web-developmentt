Richmond, Virginia

Richmond is the capital city of Virginia. Richmond is the perfect city to visit if you are passionate about history, arts, gardens, antiques, fine dining, outdoor recreations, theme parks, or NASCAR. Richmond features one of the most popular fine art museums, the VMFA (Virginia Museum of Fine Arts). There are also thousands of acres of parks throughout Richmond. The James and Appomatox river being nearby allows for several water activities to be had whether its swimming, boating, canoeing, or fishing. The city of richmond was incorporated in the year 1737. ("Richmond - Virginia Is for Lovers," n.d.)

Richmond, Virginia, has a population of 226,610 people as of the 2020 U.S Census ("Explore census data," n.d.).

The region that Richmond is located in is the Piedmont region. Richmond is urban, suburban, and rural ("Our region | plan rva," 2019)

Richmond's average household salary is $58,988 yearly, compared to Virginia's average salary of $85,873 ("Explore census data," n.d.).
